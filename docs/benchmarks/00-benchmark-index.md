## How Prototype Prompts Should Use These Docs

1. Read `06-cross-site-patterns.md` first.
2. Read the site-specific benchmark most relevant to the concept.
3. Extract layout, content hierarchy, CTA, trust, and operator-management patterns.
4. Do not copy brand identity, exact colors, exact layout, text, images, logos, or product descriptions.
5. Convert patterns into original prototype decisions.

## Benchmark To Prototype Mapping

| Prototype Concept | Primary References | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Traditional Trust | Hyecho Travel, Sonan Travel | Deep information structure, trust signals, product operations |
| Premium Custom | Small Star Tour, The Road To India | Specialty positioning, curated destination appeal |
| Product Marketplace | Sonan Travel, Hyecho Travel | Product cards, dates, prices, categories, search/list behavior |
| Consultation Landing | Oji Tour, Small Star Tour | Consultation-first specialty travel and clear lead capture |
| Content/Story | Small Star Tour, Sonan Travel, The Road To India | Destination storytelling, guide expertise, long-haul trust building |

## Review Rule

Every generated prototype should explain:

- Which benchmark patterns were used.
- Which patterns were intentionally avoided.
- How the design stays original.
- Whether the result supports a non-developer operator.
