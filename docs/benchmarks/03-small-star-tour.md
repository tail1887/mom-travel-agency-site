# 03. Small Star Tour Benchmark

Source: https://smallstartour.com/

## Positioning

Small Star Tour uses an emotional and specific positioning around elegant travel for adults. The site emphasizes long-haul destinations such as South America, Africa, the Middle East, India, Central Asia, Mongolia, and polar regions.

## Useful Patterns

### Clear Brand Phrase

- The phrase "elegant travel for adults" gives the site a memorable identity.
- This shows the value of a simple positioning line that customers can repeat.

### Destination-Led Visual Browsing

The home page exposes many destination tiles and seasonal product links:

- South America
- Africa
- Middle East
- India
- Indian Ocean
- Caucasus
- Central Asia
- Iceland
- Norway
- Mongolia
- Central America
- Australia/New Zealand

This works well when customers browse by dream destination rather than exact date.

### Trust Footer

The footer includes strong trust information:

- Representative phone number
- Company name and representative
- Business registration number
- Tourism business number
- Guarantee insurance
- Travel liability insurance
- Mail contacts
- Office hours
- Address
- Official deposit account
- Terms and privacy links

This is highly relevant for a travel agency because customers often need reassurance before contacting or depositing money.

### Inquiry Convenience

The page exposes phone and Kakao-related contact paths, which supports consultation-driven operation.

## Design Implications

- A short, memorable brand line can carry the site.
- Use destination cards with strong imagery.
- Include trust/legal/insurance information in a visible footer.
- Keep phone and Kakao consultation persistent or repeated.

## Operator Implications

Admin-managed areas may include:

- Destination categories
- Featured products
- Seasonal departure collections
- Consultation link
- Footer business/trust information
- Product explanation session links

## Patterns To Avoid

- Avoid overwhelming the first page with too many destination tiles before the user understands the agency.
- Avoid decorative emotion without enough product and trust detail.

## Prototype Usage

Best suited for:

- Premium custom prototype
- Content/story prototype
- Traditional trust prototype
